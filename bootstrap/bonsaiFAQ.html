Will weather affect the shipment of my order?

We ship year-round. When temperatures are below freezing, we use heat packs to ensure tropical product arrives safely. In rare instances of extreme cold, we may advise that some shipments be delayed or shipped Federal Express Saver, instead of our usual FedEx Ground shipping service. This is necessary for some sensitive plant material. In these rare instances, we will contact you directly to make arrangements.

RETURNS

For Shipping/Delivery Errors, Missing Items, Damage Due to Shipping/Delivery

We take great pride in our quality and preparation for the shipping process. In the event your tree or products are damaged during transit or you feel that quality is an issue, please contact us within 5 days of arrival at orders@brusselsbonsai.com. We will be happy to assist and guide you through our simple claim process. Our support team will respond to your request within 1-2 business days and offer a resolution or request additional information. Or, speak to someone directly by calling 1-800-582-2593, Monday - Friday, 8:00am - 4:30pm (CST).

For All Other Return Requests

We will gladly replace product or refund the purchase price on claims made within 30 days of your receipt. If there is a problem with your order, email us at orders@brusselsbonsai.com. We will respond to your request within 1-2 business days and offer a resolution or request additional information. You can also give us a call Monday - Friday, 8:00am - 4:30pm (CST) at 1-800-582-2593.
